About Institute of Digital Risk

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led initiative focused on developing talent in cybersecurity, AI risk and digital governance.

Through partnerships with universities and global organisations, IDR bridges the gap between academic research and real-world industry needs.
